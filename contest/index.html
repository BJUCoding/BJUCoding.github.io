Register

3 Steps to Register

The Registration process works as follows:

  1. Register for an account on spoj.com, and solve this problem using one of the following languages: Python 3, Java, C#, Visual Basic.NET, or C++. Your solution must be accepted by the spoj judge in order for you to qualify to enter the contest.
  2. Fill out the registration form. We will verify your qualification, and reply by email with further instructions.
  3. Send in the $10 registration fee. Your registration is not final until we receive your fee.

Registration Deadline is Monday, March 13.
